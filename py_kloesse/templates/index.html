Kloesse
A simple web app that divides the page extent of a book into chunks of 32 pages.


Even after using this to calculate the signature distribution of a book, do check with the printers. Each printer will have their own professional imposition software which should have the final say on the spread of the signatures.
The wiki can be found here.


To start, enter the page extent of the Front Matter.

...psst, it has to be divisible by 4.


Enter the total page extent of the book (without Front Matter).
